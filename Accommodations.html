Lean-To

The Lean-To was built in the early 2000s in the Adirondack style.
It features all the latest in Lean-To comnfort:

  • A solid wood floor
  • Cracks between the logs to promote airflow
  • A wide open front for a complete lack of privacy
  • A firepit in front for warmth, but far enough away to prevent the burning down of the building
  • A spacious bathroom, AKA the woods.
