Better Living Through Lindsey

Lindsey Stirling has touched many lives through her words and music, including mine. Lindsey's life has had it's challenges, and she's used different techniques to help overcome those challenges. I haven't seen any comprehensive list of the techniques and resources she used, so I decided to assemble what I could find.
Lindsey's faith is a core component of who she is. It gives her strength in times of need, and it would be a disservice to not mention it when talking about how she overcame her depression and anorexia.
Lindsey is a Mormon, a member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints.
Techniques and Practices
The Inner Dialogue
She began to listen to her inner voice, and when she started to think negative things, would turn it around and say something positive.
Prepping for the day.
Preparing the mind for the day, the same way we prepare the body. Lindsey used Meditation and visualization.
Professional Help.
Get help from those trained to help you.
